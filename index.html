Inspirational Quotes

“Regarding the body as the Temple of God, we will be ever watchful to keep it healthy and full of vitality by adopting the principles of self-restraint, order and harmony in our daily-lives.” – Pt. Shri Ram Sharma Acharya
“With a view to keeping our minds free from the inrush of negative thoughts and emotions, we will adopt a regular program of study of ennobling and inspiring literature (swadhyaya) and of keeping the company of saints (satsang).” – Pt. Shri Ram Sharma Acharya
“We will vigilantly exercise strict control over our senses, thoughts, emotions and in the spending of our time and resources.” – Pt. Shri Ram Sharma Acharya

About Us

Shantikunj, Haridwar is the spiritual headquarters of All World Gayatri Pariwar.
Founded by Pt. Shriram Sharma Acharya, it promotes value-based living and spiritual growth through the ideals of Gayatri Mantra and Yagya.

Activities and Programs

Our Mission & Vision

Gayatri Pariwar envisions a world guided by unity, equality, and the spirit of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam—“the whole world is one family.” It strives for individual and social transformation through divinity in human life. Our mission is to foster self-growth, harmony, and service, leading to a healthy body, pure mind, and a civilized, compassionate society.

Origin of Mission

Establishing Tapobhumi, Initiating Yug Nirman Yojna.

History and Achievements

Pandit Shriram Sharma Acharya founded Gayatri Tapobhumi (1953) and launched Yug Nirman Yojna (1958)

Vichar Kranti Abhiyaan

Promoting peace and unity through shared values and collective growth.
